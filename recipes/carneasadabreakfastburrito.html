Carne Asada Breakfast Burrito

Description

How to make Carne Asada Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients

  • 1 pound flank steak.
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  • 1 teaspoon salt.
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin.
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander.
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder.
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder.
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano.
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme.
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper.
  • 1 teaspoon paprika.
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper.
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley.
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil.
  • 1 teaspoon dried marjoram.
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary.
  • 1 teaspoon dried sage.
  • 1 teaspoon dried tarragon.
  • 1 teaspoon dried savory.
  • 1 teaspoon dried mint.
  • 1 teaspoon dried chives.
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill.
  • 1 teaspoon dried cilantro.
  • 1 teaspoon dried fennel.
  • 1 teaspoon dried mustard.
  • 1 teaspoon dried tumeric.
  • 1 teaspoon dried ginger.
  • 1 teaspoon dried nutmeg.
  • 1 teaspoon dried allspice.
  • 1 teaspoon dried celery seed.
  • 1 teaspoon dried bay leaf.
  • 1 teaspoon dried lemon peel.
  • 1 teaspoon dried orange peel.
  • 1 teaspoon dried lime peel.
  • 1 teaspoon dried lemon balm.
  • 1 teaspoon dried lemon verbena.
  • 1 teaspoon dried lemon grass.
  • 1 teaspoon dried lemon myrtle.
  • 1 teaspoon dried lemon thyme.
  • 1 teaspoon dried lemon balm.
  • 1 teaspoon dried lemon verbena.

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix well. Place steak in a large resealable plastic bag with the marinade, seal, and turn to coat; refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil grate.
  3. Grill steak 5 to 7 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. Let rest 5 minutes before slicing.
